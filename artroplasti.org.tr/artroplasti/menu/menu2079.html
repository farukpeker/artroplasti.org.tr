FOR ASSOCIATION MEMBERSHIP

Click for the membership registration form (PDF) 

Required documents for application

  • 2 photographs
  • Copy of ID card
  • Orthopedics and Traumatology Specialist Certificate
  • Names and signatures of 2 referees

After filling out the Membership Form, please send the above documents to the association secretariat address.

NOTE: Do not deposit the membership fee into the bank before your membership has been approved.

Annual dues and payment

After your membership is approved, please deposit the annual due of 500 TL into the account specified below and send the receipt to the association secretariat by post or e-mail.
