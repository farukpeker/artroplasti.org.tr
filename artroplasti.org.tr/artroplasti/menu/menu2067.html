Dear Colleagues, Esteemed Visitors, and Future Orthopedic Surgeons,

Turkish Society of Hip Knee Arthroplasty is one of the most effective approaches in modern orthopedic surgery for both functional recovery and quality of life. This field is undergoing a multidimensional transformation through advances in surgical techniques, implant technology, biomechanical analysis, and especially with robot-assisted surgical systems.

As the Turkish Society of Hip Knee Arthroplasty, our core mission is to disseminate evidence-based practice, promote knowledge sharing at national and international levels, and foster a professional community grounded in ethical values.

Guided by the principle of continuity in education, we value collaboration both with expert colleagues and with physicians at every stage of training.

Every practice that adds value to public health stands on the pillars of accurate knowledge, ethical responsibility, and high-quality education. In this spirit, our website aims to be a reference not only for colleagues but also for individuals seeking reliable information about hip and knee prostheses.

Understanding innovative approaches such as robotic arthroplasty is of great importance for both patient safety and treatment success.

For our younger colleagues in training, arthroplasty will, in the decades ahead, be shaped by the most advanced technologies of surgical practice.

  • Navigation systems
  • AI-assisted planning
  • Robotic surgery applications

These technologies demand not only clinical competence but also digital literacy and adaptability. As an association, our foremost goal is to build—together with you—the scientific and practical infrastructure that will support this transformation.

We envision this platform not merely as an information source, but as a professional space open to learning together, progressing, and shaping the future.

With your contributions, we are confident that this structure will form a strong bridge between professional excellence and social benefit.

Respectfully,   
Prof. Dr. İbrahim Tuncay   
President, KADAD
