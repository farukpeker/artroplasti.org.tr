Dear Colleagues,


As the Hip and Knee Arthroplasty Association, we will hold the 25th Basic Arthroplasty Course on May 30–31, 2025 at The Ankara Hotel. Aimed at young colleagues who wish to improve themselves in hip and knee arthroplasty, this basic course intends to update knowledge and skills not only through theoretical training but also via practical applications on models and interactive case discussions at round tables. With experienced instructors in arthroplasty, participants will have the opportunity for both practical and theoretical discussions. 
An updated, interactive program has been planned for our two-day course. We will be happy to share our experiences with you. 
With our kind regards, we look forward to meeting at the 25th Basic Arthroplasty Course.


President, Hip and Knee Arthroplasty Association: Prof. Dr. İbrahim Tuncay 
Course Chair: Assoc. Prof. Dr. Hakan Kocaoğlu
