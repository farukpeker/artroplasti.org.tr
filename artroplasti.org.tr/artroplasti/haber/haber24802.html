Basic Arthroplasty Course — Successfully Completed
October 10–11, 2025 · Acıbadem Maslak Hospital · İstanbul

Our Basic Arthroplasty Course was successfully held on October 10–11, 2025, at Acıbadem Maslak Hospital, with the participation of 70 trainees and 46 faculty members.

The course, which attracted great interest, featured hands-on practice on artificial bones, interactive roundtable discussions, and engaging video presentations.

Throughout the two-day event, participants had the opportunity to interact one-on-one with esteemed professors and ask questions directly. The course concluded successfully, supported by the participants’ positive and sincere feedback.

Best regards,
KADAD
70 trainees · 46 faculty · 2025

Dear Colleagues,


As the Hip and Knee Arthroplasty Association, we will hold the 26th Basic Arthroplasty Course on October 10–11, 2025 at Istanbul Acıbadem Maslak Hospital. Aimed at young colleagues who wish to improve themselves in hip and knee arthroplasty, this basic course intends to update knowledge and skills through not only theoretical training but also practical applications on models and interactive case discussions at round tables. With experienced instructors in arthroplasty, participants will have the opportunity for both practical and theoretical discussions.  
An updated, interactive program is planned for our two-day course. We will be pleased to share our experiences with you.  
With our kind regards, we look forward to meeting at the 26th Basic Arthroplasty Course.


President, Hip and Knee Arthroplasty Association: Prof. Dr. İbrahim Tuncay  
Course Chair: Prof. Dr. Vahit Emre Özden
