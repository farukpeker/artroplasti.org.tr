19th Advanced Arthroplasty Course: A Meeting of Knowledge and Experience in Baku

On September 26–27, 2025, the 19th Advanced Arthroplasty Course was held in Azerbaijan’s capital, Baku, hosted by the Marriott Hotel. With the participation of 90 trainees and 50 academics, the event offered attendees a rich experience through an intensive scientific program.

Throughout the course, interactive roundtable sessions—where our distinguished professors shared their knowledge and experience—drew great interest. These meetings provided a productive environment in which participants could seek answers to their questions while also exchanging ideas. In addition, the sawbones practical sessions conducted under the supervision of our professors further enhanced the value of the course by turning theoretical knowledge into practice.

These two days in Baku concluded in an atmosphere filled with scientific depth and sincere exchange. The 19th Advanced Arthroplasty Course left a lasting impression on participants as both an instructive and inspiring event.
